Splash Ice — Full-Funnel Growth Engine

Solo-Operated Growth Function · Acquisition, Retention, Lifecycle, Analytics

Operating as a fully autonomous individual contributor, I own the entire growth function for Splash Ice — no team to hand work off to, no one else's roadmap to lean on. That meant building the lifecycle and retention program in Klaviyo and Mailchimp from scratch, running paid acquisition across Google Ads and Meta Ads, and using GA to separate what was actually converting from what just looked good.

Over successive phases of the brand's growth, I redesigned the retention system and consolidated underperforming channels, reallocating spend toward what the data showed was working. I also led the people side of execution — contractors, street teams, and promotion crews — plus cross-industry brand collaborations spanning food, automotive, fine art, hospitality, and education.

The result: sustained revenue growth and meaningfully lower operating costs, achieved through funnel discipline rather than added headcount or spend.
